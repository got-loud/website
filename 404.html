The Initiative

Your samples, high grade swag and strain cards will be included in our monthly sample boxes which will be sent out to head buyers for the main chain retail stores in Ontario. You will receive feedback regarding the samples directly from the head buyers. Depending on the package you choose you could also be eligible to receive partial / full brand account management at the key accounts level.

BUYER’S CLUB SAMPLE BOX - $500

Your samples, high end swag and strain cards will be mailed out on a monthly basis to head buyers from Ontario Retail stores. You will receive feedback from the head buyers.

BRAND ASSISTANT KEY ACCOUNTS MANAGEMENT & SAMPLE BOX - $1000

Your brand will be showcased to head buyers weekly in a news letter, your products will be included in our monthly sample box, your team be provided introductions with head buyers as you see fit, relationships will be strengthened between your brands and all head buyers for chain stores as a result of this program.

KEY ACCOUNTS MANAGEMENT & SAMPLE BOX - $1500

Your brand will be showcased to head buyers weekly in a news letter, your products will be included in our monthly sample box, your team be provided introductions with head buyers as you see fit, relationships will be strengthened between your brands and all head buyers for chain stores as a result of this program. Your brand will receive full coverage and communication with all head buyers, all pay to play options will be brought to your attention, social media coverage will be provided as part of this package.
